G.A.M.A. is my Final Year Project: an AI-powered nutrition app designed to make healthy eating practical, affordable, and sustainable. Instead of giving generic diet charts, G.A.M.A. generates personalized meal plans based on user goals (fat loss, muscle gain, better energy), dietary preferences, and daily routine.

The system helps users with:

- goal-based meal recommendations and calorie guidance
- photo-based calorie estimation for local Pakistani dishes
- AI exercise plan generation aligned with nutrition goals
- macro-aware planning for protein, carbs, and fats
- adaptive suggestions based on progress and feedback
- meal history tracking to review eating patterns over time

The core idea is to turn nutrition into a guided decision system rather than a one-time plan. With AI-driven personalization, G.A.M.A. aims to support better long-term habits and make nutrition coaching accessible to more people.
